Animals in Entertainment

Many animals are use in circuses, zoos, movies, etc, as entertainment for people. These animals include tigers lions, bears, dolphins, monkeys giraffes, etc. Being kept in Zoos, many animal lost their ability to perform some necessary skills needed to survive in the wild. One study have shown that when release after being kept for long period of time, only 33 % of the captive-bred carnivores survived.

Source: Sentient Media

Taking photos with tigers

Do you know what a tiger went through just for you to take a photo with them?

Tigers are taken from their mothers at only a few weeks old, and bred in industrial tiger farms. They have to go through strict and cruel training and adult tigers have to be chained inorder to be taken photos with. When they aren't being taken photos of, they are led back into their cages in which they are packed together without enough room.

Source: World Animal Protection
